Zer0

I mained as Axton when I was playing Borderlands 2 with my friends, but my true love is Zer0. If you want to know anything about me at all, you should know that my ideal self is a faceless, generless robot that emotes solely through an electric display (as does the Tiny Astronaut). I don't have the body type to properly cosplay Zer0, so I just stuck with the helmet.

Skills Learned

My friend recently bought a 3D printer (that promptly committed suicide after less than 72 hours after unboxing). I knew he'd get another one, so I began plans to print a model of Zer0's helmet. I learned to used Blender, a really useful 3D modeling software, as well as how to print using FDM and SLA printers.
