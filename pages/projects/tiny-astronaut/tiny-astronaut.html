Tiny Astronaut

Two things about me: (1) I am a really short person who likes tiny things, like eating from tiny bowls with tiny spoons and tiny forks, and (2) I have been obsessed with space since I was three. Naturally, I was drawn to The Tiny Astronaut. Tiny Astronaut is one of the "stickers" (sort of like emoji, but bigger and more detailed) available in Google Hangouts. I've been wanting to dress up as this incredibly cute astronaut of indeterminate species and gender for a couple years. It certainly is a deviation from my traditional Halloween theme of "otherworldly and vaguely threatening," but being tiny astronaut is too cute to pass up.
