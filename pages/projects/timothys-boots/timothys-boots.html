Timothy's Boots

A few years ago, I received a rather incoherent message from a client; the fact that they addressed me as Timothy, a name not even close to my actual name, was the least strange aspect of it. I had been eating dinner with Rowan and his parents at the time and decided to narrate the email to them. Everybody thought it was hilarious and started calling me Timothy. The joke went on for weeks before it all came to a head. My a professor was calling roll and said "Timothy" and I was only a moment of clarity away from saying, "Here." I told this to Rowan, who then relayed it to his parents, and really sealed my new name as Timothy among my close friends.

Which is why I was obligated to play Timothy in the Borderlands pre-sequel.

Anyways, I'm a huge sucker for gold/silver accents on otherwise normal pieces of attire. It's arguable that the yellow bits of Timtam's boots are just flat leather/metal, but Jack is extra as fuck, and I'm convinced he would absolutely have gold heeled boots. Unfortunately, I don't wear many outfits that go with brown and gold, so I'm changing up the color scheme a bit here. Just pretend this is a skin for him. I couldn't decide between black/gold or black/silver, and went, "Why not both?" So these boots let you change the color of the accents depending on the rest of your outfit! Awesome!

Construction

Gearbox provided actual cosplay reference material, which was a great help.

Riding Boots

I had been been waiting over a year for the perfect pair of riding boots to drop at my local thrift shop, but nothing came, so I ended up buying them at full price. I knew these would be daily wear, so I didn't mind spending a bit more on them. Although, I didn't really need any justification for them; my cosplay spending budget is literally the embodiment of this tweet.

In addition to the gold and silver options, I wanted to be able to go accent free. This meant that the attachment method would have to be either discreet or presentable if the metal plates weren't there. Because I couldn't come up with a good way, I added another accent option in black. They're all attachable via several thin strips of velcro.

Metal Heel

Now, I was tempted to go for artificial gold/silver colored cloth, but thought that was lame and boring. Let's chrome and gold plate actual metal!
