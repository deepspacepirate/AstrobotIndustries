Hi, I'm Watney!

I'm an electronics hobbyist, chemist, costume maker, scifi/fantasy writer, digital artist, and amateur astronomer.

Wow! Where did you learn all of that?

Save for chemistry and astronomy, everything is self taught. I like learning.

My "official" education is a BS in biochemistry from CSULA in 2014. I also completed a thesis, "The Role of Wdr68 in Bmp Signaling" under the supervision of Dr. Robert Nissen.

Aren't you a tech person? Why a biochemistry degree?

Many years ago, I thought I wanted to go to medical school, so I took the MCAT and GRE, submitted applications, and got as far as getting interviews before deciding I didn't really want to do that.

So I went back to school to learn about computers! I'm currently a full time student working towards a degree in Computer Science.

Don't get me wrong though; I have an immense love for chemistry! Astrobiology is my favorite. Many of the videos on my YouTube channel, NKLOK Labs, focus heavily on chemistry.

What kind of work do you do?

I mainly tutor in a number of STEM subjects. I also do some consulting and contracting work, mostly programming.

What do you do for fun?

Pretty much everything I do is for fun, even school; I wouldn't doing it if it wasn't!
